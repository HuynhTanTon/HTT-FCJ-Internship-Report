### What I learned (as an attendee)

#### Soft skills & experience sharing (from The Ballers)

- Public sharing needs a **clear structure** and concise idea delivery.
- Teamwork under deadline requires clear task split and continuous communication.

#### Practical learning path (from Huynh Thai Linh)

- Learn cloud in order: **architecture mindset → code & fast testing → real production**, instead of jumping straight to the console.
- Cloud Quest builds “mind” first; Floci supports fast iteration; real AWS comes last.
- Following Huynh Thai Linh on LinkedIn extends learning beyond the room.

#### FCAJ community perspective

- The Bitexco sharing combined **project experience** and a **learning roadmap** — well suited for an intern working on a workshop / report.

### Application to the internship

- Map the 3 phases onto the URL Shortener: grasp architecture → code/test Lambda–DynamoDB–S3 → deploy and operate on real AWS.
- Practice explaining architecture briefly and clearly, as the speakers did.
- Keep a fast-iterate mindset when finishing the workshop and report.

### Personal experience

As an **attendee**, I followed two consecutive sharings on Floor 26 of Bitexco: real build-under-pressure experience, plus a clear learning roadmap. The two angles complement each other and help shape my FCAJ/AWS internship direction.

> Overall, the speaker sharing helped me learn how to communicate experience and gave me a practical cloud learning frame to apply to my serverless project and internship report.
